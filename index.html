About the Club

Ardmore Cricket Club has been a pillar of community sport in the North West for generations. Based at the historic Bleachgreen ground, the club fields teams across senior and youth levels and proudly competes in the North West Cricket Union. We are passionate about providing a welcoming environment for everyone to enjoy cricket — whether you are taking guard in the middle, helping out behind the scenes, or cheering from the boundary.

Our volunteers, coaches, and committee members work year-round to grow the game locally, nurture young talent, and provide memorable matchday experiences for supporters. If you are new to cricket or returning after a break, there is a place for you at Ardmore CC.

Teams & Programmes

  • 1st XI competing in North West Championship fixtures.
  • 2nd & 3rd XIs providing a pathway for emerging senior players.
  • Youth teams at under-11, under-13, and under-15 levels.
  • Girls and women’s softball cricket initiatives each summer.
  • "Smash It" (Ages 5-9) summer programme and community taster sessions for beginners.

Training Nights

Senior squad: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 6:30 pm

Youth squads: Fridays, 6:00 pm

Training takes place at the Bleachgreen. New players are encouraged to arrive a few minutes early so we can introduce you to coaches and teammates.

Join Ardmore CC

Membership is open to players of all abilities as well as social supporters. Annual subscriptions help us maintain the facilities, provide equipment, and sustain youth development programmes.

Please contact any committee member, message us on our socials or email us at ardmorecc@gmail.com for more information. We are happy to answer any questions you may have about fees, training, or match availability.

Membership Rates 2026

  • Senior player: £120
  • Student / Apprentice: £70
  • Youth player: £0
  • Social member: £25

Flexible payment plans are available. Please speak to the club treasurer for details.

Plan Your Visit

The Bleachgreen sits just outside the village of Ardmore, approximately 6 miles from Derry~Londonderry city centre. Matchday parking is available on-site.

Refreshments are served from the licensed bar in the clubhouse during senior fixtures, and supporters can enjoy excellent views from the pavilion or grass banking around the ground. Dogs are welcome but should remain on leads at all times.
