About the Club

Ardmore Cricket Club has been a pillar of community sport in the North West for generations. Based at the historic Bleachgreen ground, the club fields teams across senior and youth levels and proudly competes in North West Cricket Union competitions. We are passionate about providing a welcoming environment for everyone to enjoy cricket — whether you are taking guard in the middle, helping out behind the scenes, or cheering from the boundary.

Our volunteers, coaches, and committee members work year-round to grow the game locally, nurture young talent, and provide memorable matchday experiences for supporters. If you are new to cricket or returning after a break, there is a place for you at Ardmore CC.

Teams & Programmes

  • 1st XI competing in North West Premiership fixtures.
  • 2nd XI providing a pathway for emerging senior players.
  • Youth teams at under-11, under-13, and under-15 levels.
  • Girls and women’s softball cricket initiatives each summer.
  • All Stars Cricket and community taster sessions for beginners.

Training Nights

Senior squad: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 6:30 pm

Youth squads: Fridays, 6:00 pm

Training takes place at the Bleachgreen. New players are encouraged to arrive a few minutes early so we can introduce you to coaches and teammates.

Join Ardmore CC

Membership is open to players of all abilities as well as social supporters. Annual subscriptions help us maintain the facilities, provide equipment, and sustain youth development programmes.

Download our membership form, complete your details, and return it to any committee member or email it to ardmorecc@gmail.com. We are happy to answer any questions you may have about fees, training, or match availability.

Membership Rates 2024

  • Senior player: £120
  • Student / Apprentice: £70
  • Youth player: £40
  • Family (2 adults + juniors): £180
  • Social member: £25

Flexible payment plans are available. Please speak to the club treasurer for details.

Latest News

1st XI Secure Opening Day Victory

Ardmore’s senior side opened the season with a convincing win at the Bleachgreen, chasing down 187 with seven wickets in hand. Skipper’s half-century and disciplined bowling from the seam attack set the tone for a promising campaign.

Junior Coaching Returns

Friday night coaching sessions for juniors resume in May. Qualified coaches deliver fun, skills-based drills designed to develop confidence for match play. Parents and guardians are welcome to stay for refreshments in the clubhouse.

Volunteers Needed

We are recruiting additional scorers, grounds volunteers, and matchday helpers. If you can lend a hand for even a few hours a month, please contact the committee — every bit of support helps our teams thrive.

Plan Your Visit

The Bleachgreen sits just outside the village of Ardmore, approximately 6 miles from Derry~Londonderry city centre. Matchday parking is available on-site with overflow options in the village. Please follow steward directions and park responsibly.

Refreshments are served from the clubhouse kiosk during senior fixtures, and supporters can enjoy excellent views from the pavilion or grass banking around the ground. Dogs are welcome but should remain on leads at all times.
